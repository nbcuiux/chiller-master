Imagery

The imagery used on ChillerTV.com will be dark and ominous, reminding the viewer of the fear and horror that they feel from watching Chiller. The use of large unknown areas and decrepit environments will help reinforce this feeling.

To keep with the dark ominous feeling, we have taken the image that had its vibrancy altered and added some distressing and weathering to it. By softening the colors as well as using a darker blue-green tone, it not only increases the dark and ominous feel but also makes it easier for text on the image to be read.
