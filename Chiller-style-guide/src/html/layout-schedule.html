Schedule

The schedule page will be used to simply present the viewer with the latest schdule of shows on ChillerTV.com

On this page the viewer will be presented with a listing of all upcoming shows on ChillerTV.com They have the ability change their time zone, dates, as well as filter by show. Below this seciton the viewer will be presented with a list of the shows which will include a thumbnail image, title of the show, small description of the episode, season and episode number, time it will be shown, and options for viewing online or on apps if available.

If the viewer chooses to scroll through the page instead of using the filtering option the additional shows will transition into the page from top to bottom. An example of a similar animation can be found here.
