Videos

Videos can be used to add more of an immersive feel when using hero graphics. Videos also will be used in a video player for viewers to watch their favorite episodes directly on ChillerTV.com.

Contents

  • Hero
  • Player

Hero

Adding subtle videos used as a hero can add to the eerie and ominous feeling for the viewer.

Player

The video player for ChillerTV.com will be simple and elegant for the user to view. It fill the users entire screen with the acception of the header and footer. If a use hovers over the video, player controls will then appear.

The viewer will have the ability to navigate to any episode from that season of that series by simply clicking the "epsidoes" indicator at the bottom of the video player. This will then slide up a panel that will show three episodes at a time. If the user wants to go further than the next three episodes they will have the ability to scroll left or right in this panel.
