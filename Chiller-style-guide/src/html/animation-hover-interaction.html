Hover Interactions

Hover animations are a great interactive element to add to your website. Hover animations are not only visual appealing but serve a purpose too. A hover animation can also be used to show indicate to the viewer that an elements is clickable or reveal additional information.

Contents

  • Background
  • Reveals
  • Text & Logos
  • Buttons & Links

Background Animations

The use of background image animations will reinforce dark oninous feeling given off by the Chiller brand. These animations are ideal for tile backgrounds or hero graphics. Background animations should will be subtle additions to strong imagery.

Reveals

Reveal animations provide viewers with a visual appealing way to show or hide more information. These animations are ideal to show a quick synopsis for an episode or photo.

Text Animations

Similar to background animations, subtle text and logos animations can be used to make the viewer aware they have hovered over an actionable item. For ChillerTV.com the slice animation is subtle and consistent with Chiller branding.

Buttons

Similarly to the tile press, the buttons will have the same press animation.
