Cards & Tiles

Cards and tiles are used to help organize and arrange content on a website. For ChillerTV.com we will be using cards and tiles for a majority of our content as shown in the wireframes found in the layout section. The major difference between cards and tiles is that cards can display both a image section as well as a text section where tiles should only display images or videos.

Cards

Cards will be used to show content on as the actor biographys, news, and social media updates.

Tiles

Tiles will be used to show content such as photos, videos, shows, characters, and advertisments.
